WoW Kababs

This kabab is super flavorful, tender, and delicious. You can make it with ingredients that are easily available in your kitchen. Seekh kababs are so versatile that you can serve them with a dip, on a bed of rice, or stuff them in rolls.

  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, salt, pepper)

Method

  1. Cut the chicken and vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Prepare the marinade by mixing olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and spices.
  3. Marinate the chicken and vegetables for at least 1 hour.
  4. Thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers over medium-high heat for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
